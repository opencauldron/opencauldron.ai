---
title: Brand Management
description: Organize generations by brand and maintain creative consistency.
---

Brands let you organize your generated media into logical groups. Tag generations with a brand to keep projects and clients separate in the gallery.

## Creating a brand

Navigate to **Brands** in the sidebar and click **New Brand**. Give it a name and choose a color to distinguish it in the UI.

## Using brands

When generating images or videos, select a brand from the dropdown. All generations are tagged with the selected brand, making it easy to:

- Filter the gallery by brand
- Track how many assets belong to each project or client

## What brands store

A brand has a name and a color. The color appears as a visual label throughout the gallery and makes it easy to distinguish assets at a glance. Brands do not define default models, prompt prefixes, or any generation settings — they are purely a tagging and organizational tool.
